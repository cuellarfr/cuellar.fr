About Me

Hello, this is Carlos Cuéllar. Let me introduce myself. I’m a certified User Experience Designer with an industrial design background and some love for all tech things. When not staring at a screen, I listen to classic alternative rock music records and I try to find good food in downtown Portland.

Now, the work stuff. I’ve been creating digital experiences that simplify user’s lives since 2006. I started working at marketing agencies in Spain, and after moving to the US in 2011 I became an in-house UX Designer specialized in B2B applications. I currently work at Trimble Inc. where we craft software solutions for construction companies.

Originally from Valencia, Spain, I live now in the Portland area where I'm raising my two kids.
